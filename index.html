About Me

I’m a final-year MSc in Human-Computer Interaction student, researching user-friendly interfaces for quantum computing applications at the intersection of finance and technology. Proficient in Qiskit, Figma, and Plotly, I aim to bridge quantum complexity with intuitive design for industry leaders like TQA, Equal1, and Fidelity Investments. My thesis explores enhancing accessibility of quantum tools for non-experts, combining technical expertise with HCI principles to drive innovation in quantum finance and business solutions.
